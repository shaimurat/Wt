Dishonored Game series

Dishonored is an immersive first-person action game that casts you as a supernatural assassin driven by revenge.

Main characters

Corvo Attano

Corvo Attano is the protagonist of Dishonored and one of the two playable protagonists of Dishonored 2. Previously the Royal Protector and secret paramour to Empress Jessamine Kaldwin

Emily Kaldwin

Emily Kaldwin is the daughter of Corvo.She is playable in Dishonored 2. She is the rightful heir to the throne of the Isles and Empress Jessamine Kaldwin's only child.
